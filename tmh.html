ABOUT THE ORGANIZATION: THE MIAMI HURRICANE

The Miami Hurriane (TMH) is the official student newspaper of the University of Miami and was founded in 1929.

While the newspaper was widely circulated in the past, the emergence of the digital age and the coronavirus pandemic has created an emphasis on online journalism within the organization.

Now, TMH publishes the majority of their content on their website and on their digital newsletters.

However, in the Fall 2020 semester, the newspaper did distribute a print edition themed after the presidential election. These special edition newspapers are likely to be printed more in the future as less standard issues are released.

The paper spans their journalistic coverage across a variety of sections, including news, opinion, culture and sports.

MY INVOLVEMENT

I joined The Miami Hurricane in my freshman fall semester as a reporter for both the sports and news teams. Coming into college, I knew I wanted to be a part of the newspaper staff given how much I enjoyed and grew from my time with CavsConnect during high school.

After a year of writing with the paper, I have enjoyed the experience just as much, and I have published over 25 articles combined across the two sections.
