ABOUT THE ORGANIZATION: CATEGORY 5

Category 5 is the University of Miami's Spirit Programming Board and serves to link the student body with Hurricanes Athletics through gameday events, watch parties, giveaways and more.

On-campus, Category 5 seeks to preserve and promote the university's traditions, sports culture, and passion for The U.

MY INVOLVEMENT

During my freshman orientation, I was first exposed to Category 5 through a virtual presentation on student organizations. As soon as I learned what it was all about, I applied immediately.

The energy and spirit that characterizes Category 5 really appealed to me, and as somebody that wants to work in sports media in the future, I thought it would be a great opportunity for me to learn some sports marketing skills and improve my photography and videography techniques.

Shortly after applications were due, I was accepted into the Category 5 Council under the Creative Services Committee and was assigned to the Photo / Video team.

Throughout my first year on Council, I attended numerous spirit events themed after the various sports offered at the University of Miami, including football, basketball and baseball. At each of these, I took photos and videos that were then published on the Category 5 Instagram and Facebook pages!
